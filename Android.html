WHAT'S THE LATEST VERSION OF ANDROID?

The Latest Version of Android is 15.0

Android 15 is the latest version of Android, and it was released on September 3, 2024. It will arrive first on Google Pixel phones but should quickly roll out to Samsung Galaxy devices as a beta. Phones from OnePlus, Xiaomi, Nokia, and others will eventually follow as well. Following in the tradition started with Android 10, Android 15 does not have a fun dessert nickname. However, the internal codename for the release is "Vanilla Ice Cream." It's a little sad that Google doesn't use these nicknames publicly anymore. Some of the bigger features include Private Space, Satellite Connectivity, and Adaptive Timeout.
